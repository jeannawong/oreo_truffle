        <h1>
        Oreo Truffles
        </h1>
            
        <p>
        Below are the ingredients to make the perfect Oreo Truffle balls.
        </p>
            
        <h2>
        Ingredients
        </h2>
        <ul>
        <li>1 pack of regular oreos</li>
        <li>1 8oz tub of cream cheese</li>
        <li>milk chocolage almond bark**</li>
        <li>white chocolate almond bark**</li>
        </ul>
        <p>**Note: Click on the picture below to see which almond bark brand I like to use and where to buy:</p>
        <a href=https://www.walmart.com/ip/Plymouth-Pantry-Almond-Bark-Chocolate-Baking-Bar-24-oz/10313776><img src=https://i5.walmartimages.com/asr/a4e55f72-1ba4-4348-87b7-3e0e1008cb9c_1.6ed2ae97061d4d9442623f66ee0fa0b8.jpeg></a>
        
        <h2>
        Materials
        </h2>
        <ul>
        <li>1 freezer bag</li>
        <li>rolling pin</li>
        <li>1 mixing bowl and mixing spoon</li>
        <li>2 pots</li>
        <li>toothpicks</li>
        <li>tray and wax paper</li>
        <li>sandwich bag or icing bag</li>
        </ul>
            
            
        <h2>
        Directions
        </h2>
        
        <h3>
        Creating the Balls
        </h3>
        <ol>
        <li>Place the pack of Oreos in the freezer bag, seal the freezer bag, and crush thoroughly with rolling pin.</li>
        <li>In the mixing bowl, mix together crushed oreos and the tub of cream cheese with the mixing spoon.</li>
        <li>Using clean hands, take some of the mixture and roll it into a truffle-sized ball. Place on wax paper on tray.</li>
        <li>Repeat Step 3 until all of mixture is used.</li>
        <li>Place in refrigerator to solidify for 15 minutes.</li>
        </ol>
        
        <h3>
        Making the Truffles
        </h3>
        <ol>
        <li>Melt milk chocolate almond bark in a pot.</li>
        <li>Take out tray of oreo balls from refrigerator after cooling for 15 minutes.</li>
        <li>Using the toothpicks, pick up and dip each oreo ball in the melted almond bark until fully covered. Place back on wax paper on tray.</li>
        <li>After dipping all oreo balls, place tray back in refrigerator for 20 minutes to solidify.</li>
        <li>Melt white chocolate almond bark in a pot.</li>
        <li>After 20 minutes, take out oreo truffles.</li>
        <li>Pour melted white chocolate almond bark into sandwich or icing bag. Cut a corner off the sandwich bag or cut the tip of the icing bag. Drizzle melted white chocolage bark onto oreo truffles.</li>
        <li>Refrigerate oreo truffles overnight. They will be ready for consumption.</li>
        </ol>
        
        <h3>
        Enjoy your oreo truffles!!
        </h3>
        <p>*Just as a note, oreo truffles can be made with all different types of dippings and toppings. Try it out!*</p>
